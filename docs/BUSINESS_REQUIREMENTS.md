### 🏢 Kompaniya Haqida
"ABC Retail" — bu turli hududlarda kantselyariya mollari (office supplies), mebellar (furniture) va texnologiya mahsulotlarini (technology products) sotadigan chakana savdo kompaniyasi.

Kompaniya rahbariyati o'tgan davrdagi savdo ma'lumotlarini tahlil qilish orqali biznes samaradorligini oshirishni maqsad qilgan.

---

### 🎯 Biznes Maqsadlari
Kompaniya rahbariyati quyidagi savollarga aniq javob olishni talab qilmoqda:

- Qaysi mahsulotlar eng yuqori savdo hajmini hosil qiladi?
- Qaysi mahsulotlar eng ko'p sof foyda keltirmoqda?
- Qaysi hududlar (regions) yaxshi natija ko'rsatmoqda?
- Qaysi shtatlar (states) past natija ko'rsatmoqda (underperforming)?
- Chegirmalar (discounts) foyda miqdoriga qanday ta'sir qilyapti?
- Eng qimmatli va sodiq mijozlar (valuable customers) kimlar?
- Qaysi yetkazib berish turlari (shipping methods) eng ko'p ishlatiladi?
- Oylik va yillik savdo tendensiyalari (sales trends) qanday ko'rinishga ega?

---

### 📦 Kutilayotgan Natija
Ushbu tahlil rahbariyatga biznesni rivojlantirish va to'g'ri strategik qarorlar qabul qilish uchun amaliy tavsiyalar (actionable insights) berishi kerak.

**Yakuniy topshiriladigan ishlar (Deliverables):**
- SQL yordamida chuqur tahlil (SQL Analysis)
- Excel interaktiv dashboard'i
- Power BI interaktiv dashboard'i
- Biznes uchun tavsiyalar va xulosalar (Business Recommendations)

## 🏢 Company Background
ABC Retail is a retail company that sells office supplies, furniture, and technology products across multiple regions.

The management team wants to improve business performance by analyzing historical sales data.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The company wants to answer the following questions:

- Which products generate the highest sales?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which regions are performing well?
- Which states are underperforming?
- How do discounts affect profit?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which shipping methods are most frequently used?
- What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?

---

## 📦 Expected Outcome
The analysis should provide actionable insights that help management make better business decisions.

The final deliverables include:
- SQL Analysis
- Excel Dashboard
- Power BI Dashboard
- Business Recommendations
